Story: You are Rare, a survivor of the great apocalypse that nearly destroyed 
       all of humanity. You are defending the last stores of clean food from 
       the rabbits, who, through years of living in nuclear radiation, have
       taken the form of squares. You must use your 3 remaining auto-defense 
       turrets to stop them from contaminating them.
      
Controls: Arrow keys to move
          Space bar to place a tower
          (q) to quit

Rules: You can place a maximum of 3 towers
       You cannot place a tower too close to the edges
       You cannot place a tower within the radius of another tower
       You cannot move a tower after it is placed
       If 10 rabbits make it past your towers, you lose
